HOW TO PLAY
CHESS

The lay-out of a chess game:


To win, place the opponent's king in a position where they are:

  • Under check
  • Have no legal moves

How each chess piece behaves:

The 'King'
Moves one square in any direction

The 'Queen' (worth 9 points)
Moves any possible amount of squares in any direction

The 'Bishop'
Moves any possible amount of squares diagonally

The 'Knight'
Moves in an 'L'-shaped pattern (only piece able to jump over other pieces!)

The 'Rook'
Moves any possible amount of squares vertically/horizontally

The 'Pawn'
Moves one square in any direction
